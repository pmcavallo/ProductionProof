AutoDoc AI is a multi-agent RAG system for generating regulatory documentation. It uses 4 specialized agents: Retriever (finds relevant chunks), Analyzer (extracts requirements), Synthesizer (combines findings), Validator (verifies source fidelity). Tech stack: Python, LangChain, ChromaDB, Claude API. Achieves 100% source fidelity on 200-document test corpus. Untested on documents >50 pages or concurrent users.
